Pancakes

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
large eggs2
milk2 tablespoons
vanilla extract1/2 teaspoon
(optional) lemon zest1 teaspoon
all-purpose flour1/4 cup
vanilla extract1/2 teaspoon
baking powder1/4 teaspoon
sugar1 tablespoon
powdered sugar/syrup for serving (optional)1 tablespoon
butter/oil for cooking

Steps:

  1. Prepare the batter by separating the eggs then whisking the yolks with milk, vanilla, and lemon zest. Combine with your flour and baking powder.
  2. Beat the egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form. Then gently fold whipped egg whites into the yolk mixture.
  3. Heat the skillet. Pour batter, cook covered, then flip and cook until golden brown. Enjoy your pancakes warm with either powdered sugar or syrup.
Bulalo

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
beef marrow bones2
beef shank1 pound
onion1
garlic3 cloves
black peppercorns1 teaspoon
patis2 tablespoons
saltto taste
corn2 cobs
chayote1
baby bok choy3 (separate leaves)

Steps:

  1. Boil the meat and bones to remove blood, then scrub it clean. Return the meat and bones to the pot with onion, garlic, peppercorns, and patis. Simmer until tender.
  2. Skim off the excess fat then strain the brith. Return the meat to the broth and add in the corn and chayote. Simmer again until tender.
  3. Finally, add bok choy and salt to your liking. Serve hot with rice.
Cheesy Chicken Wraps

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
chicken breats2 large
olive oil1 tbsp.
garlic powder2 tsp.
onion powder1 tsp.
paprika1 tsp.
shredded cheddar cheese1 cup
shredded mozzarella cheese1/2 cup
flour tortillas4 large
creamy garlic sauce1/2 cup.
fresh parsleyoptional for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine diced chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Toss well to ensure all the chicken pieces are evenly coated with the seasonings.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the seasoned chicken and cook for 6-8 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and nicely browned. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. Take a tortilla and spread a generous spoonful of creamy garlic sauce evenly across its surface. Top with the cooked chicken, followed by a layer of shredded cheddar cheese and then a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  4. Fold the tortilla in half to enclose the filling. Return the folded tortilla to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the cheese has melted completely and the tortilla becomes golden brown and crispy.
Corndogs

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
lukewarm water1 cup
white/brown sugar2 tbsp.
dry yeast2 tsp.
kosher salt1/2 tsp.
all-purpose flour1 3/4 cup
uncured beef hotdogs4
wooden chopsticks4
peeled ptato1 pund; cut into 1/4 inch cubes
all-purpose wheat flour2 tbsp.
Korean bread crubms (ppang-garu)1/2 cup
corn/vegetable oil for frying
sugar, ketchup, mustard(optional)

Steps:

  1. Make the dough by combining warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit until foamy. Add salt and flour, mix well until smooth. Let rise until doubled.
  2. Prepare the potatoes and skewers by blanching the potatoes, rinsing, and drying them. Mix with the flour. You may then skewer the hotdogs.
  3. Heat your oil to 320-330°F. Coat thes skewers in batter, then roll in potatoes, then breadcrumbs. Fry for about 6-7 minutes.
  4. Optionally, you can dust the corndogs with sugar, drizzle with ketchup and mustard for taste. Serve immediately!
